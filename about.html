Our Story

Built on Trust & Consistent Delivery

Ngumba Enterprises Co. Ltd began as an authorised Safaricom Mpesa dealer and has since grown into a multi-sector enterprise serving key government and institutional clients across Kenya.

  • ✓Authorised Safaricom Mpesa Agent providing financial access to communities
  • ✓Trusted procurement partner to Kenya Seed Company and the County Government of Trans Nzoia
  • ✓Authorised dealer of Kansai Plascon Kenya Limited paints and coatings
  • ✓Rubis fuel station dealer operations delivering energy access to the region
  • ✓Construction works meeting government quality and compliance standards
  • ✓Registered member of the Kenya National Chamber of Commerce & Industry
